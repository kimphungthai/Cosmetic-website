RETURNS AND EXCHANGES POLICY FOR IN-STORE PURCHASES

 

In-Store purchases can be returned for a refund or exchange within 30 days of the date of purchase with original receipt and within the original country of purchase. Returns and exchanges are subject to the terms below.

 

 

This Returns and Exchanges Policy applies to purchases made in our brick and mortar ItsMe Cosmetics locations in the US (including US Territories) Only (collectively, "In-store"). All ItsMe Cosmetics In-store purchases may only be returned to stores within the original country of purchase. Please contact your local store for any store-specific return policy information. For information about Returns and Exchanges for online purchases, click here.

At this time, ItsMe Cosmetics international retail store locations cannot accept returns from online orders through Global-e. International online purchases cannot be returned to physical stores.

ALL RETURNED OR EXCHANGED ITEMS MUST BE UNWASHED, UNWORN, AND UNDAMAGED, WITH ALL TAGS ATTACHED, AND WITH THE ORIGINAL RECEIPT. JEWELRY MUST BE IN ITS ORIGINAL PACKAGING.

UNDERWEAR, SELECT LINGERIE AND SWIM WEAR, EARRINGS, BODY PIERCINGS, AND GIFT CARDS/ E-GIFT CARDS ARE ALL FINAL SALE ITEMS AND CANNOT BE RETURNED OR EXCHANGED, UNLESS REQUIRED BY LAW.

GIFT CARDS AND STORE CREDIT ARE NOT TRANSFERABLE AND MAY NOT BE USED TO PURCHASE ANOTHER GIFT CARD OR E-GIFT CARD. UNLESS REQUIRED BY LAW, GIFT CARDS AND STORE CREDIT CANNOT BE RETURNED FOR A REFUND OR EXCHANGE AND ARE NOT REDEEMABLE FOR CASH. IF DAMAGED, LOST OR STOLEN, GIFT CARDS CAN ONLY BE REPLACED WITH ORIGINAL PROOF OF PURCHASE FOR THE REMAINING VALUE IN OUR RECORDS AT THE TIME THE CARD IS REPORTED. THERE IS NO SALES TAX ON GIFT CARDS. FOR FULL TERMS, VISIT WWW.FOREVER21.COM.
